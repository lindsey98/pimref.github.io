PiMRef

Detecting and Explaining Ever-Evolving Phishing Emails with Knowledge-Based Invariants

Introduction

In this work, we propose the 1st reference-based phishing email detection solution, PiMRef, which

  • Debunks the disprovable claims on sender identity,
  • Highlights the call-to-action triggers on next-step engagement

PiMRef significantly outperforms

  • Feature-engineering-based (D-Fence, HelpHed)
  • LLM-based (ChatSpamdetector)
  • Rule-based (Rspamd, SpamAssassin)

baselines in terms of precision, and recall, while maintaining a runtime within 0.05 seconds.
