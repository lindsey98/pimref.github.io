How PiMRef Detects Psychological Triggers

PiMRef uses a combination of natural language processing and pattern matching to identify psychological manipulation tactics in email content. When a trigger is detected, PiMRef highlights the specific language patterns and provides context about why this language might be suspicious.

Key Detection Features:

  • Urgency indicators: Time-limited language, deadline threats
  • Authority claims: References to IT, management, official departments
  • Social pressure: Mentions of colleagues, percentages, group behavior
  • Reciprocity cues: Gifts, favors, prior assistance mentioned
  • Consistency appeals: References to past actions or preferences
  • Familiarity attempts: Overly friendly language, personal references
